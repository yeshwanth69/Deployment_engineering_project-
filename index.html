Objectives of the Project

This project is aimed to investigate the role of digital payments on the economic development of emerging nations(B.R.I.C.S countries; it is an acronym), with evidences taken from India. To achieve this goal, the specific objectives of the study will be to:

1)Examine the growth of digital payments in past years. 2)evaluate the impact of digitalization of payments on various socio-demo-economic indicators. 3)measure and analyze the relationship between rise of digital payments in emerging economies and its impact on their development.

What is Exploratory Data Analysis?

Exploratory Data Analysis refers to the critical process of performing initial investigations on data so as to discover patterns,to spot anomalies,to test hypothesis and to check assumptions with the help of summary statistics and graphical representations. In statistics, is is an approach of analyzing data sets to summarize their main characteristics, often using statistical graphics and other data visualization methods. We will be using visualization tools like Tableau, PowerBi and Weka.
